What's an online account?

By registering for an online account, you'll be able to access your LADWP service account(s) online to view and pay your bills, start and stop service, apply for rebates and programs, and view outage status of your neighborhood.

Board of Commissioners - Meeting Video & Agenda Archive


Archived videos are arranged by date, with the most recent at the top of the list. Click Video to watch the meeting with documents, or Board Agenda / Board Minutes to see just the documents. You can also search the archives by typing keywords into the Search box.

Video content in this portal is only compatible with Internet Explorer and Google Chrome. HTML5 compatible browser or the Adobe Flash Plugin may be required.

Special Note for Chrome Users:
Please press play to watch the live broadcast or recorded archive.

Next Board of Commissioners Broadcast

Board Meetings are scheduled for the 2nd and 4th Tuesday of each month and are scheduled to start at 10:00am.

LADWP welcomes and encourages public engagement. Please submit your comments on agenda items by clicking the eComments link below.
The eComments period will remain open until 6:00 p.m. the day before the scheduled meeting.

The eComment period will open when the agenda is published either Friday evening or Saturday morning.
The comment period will close on Monday at 6:00 p.m.
