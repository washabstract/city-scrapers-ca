To view the Agenda and all pertinent documents for an upcoming meeting click on Agenda under Upcoming Events.

Videos for all archived meetings are arranged by date, with the most recent at the top of the list. Minutes will be available after approval. You can also search the archives by typing keywords into the Search box. An asterisk (*) denotes that these meetings are not archived; only Agenda and/or Minutes are available.

Live and Archived video can be viewed on most desktop browsers and a range of popular mobile devices. For additional information on supported platforms, please view the minimum system requirements.

eComments is a new feature available on City Council, Planning Board and Parks and Recreation Board meetings. eComments provides you the opportunity to comment on specific items up for discussion on these agendas. Once the agenda for that meeting body has posted, click on the eComments link next to the agenda link to leave a comment on specific items on that agenda. Click here for a user guide and brief video for more information.

Members of the public are encouraged to attend public meetings of the City Council and the City's Boards, Commissions and Committees. The City Council generally meets on Tuesdays at 6:00pm. Auxiliary aids and services are available for individuals with speech, vision, or hearing impairments (48-hour notice is required.)
